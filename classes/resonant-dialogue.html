Triundra Simulation — AI Answer Structure

Layer Reference

Layer Name Symbol Meaning Color

LMA — Logic-Modulated Answers

Choose a question & configure your answer by switching Logic, Emotion, Resonance layers on/off.

🔷 Basic Triundra Formula

Triundra = L + E + R

L — Logic Layer (🔵), E — Emotion Layer (🟢), R — Resonance Layer (🟣)
💡 This is not an arithmetic sum but a cognitive-semantic integration. The system activates, weighs, and blends these three layers into a unified response.

In the visual interface, this can appear as a white glow or a neutral color with subtle undertones.

🔶 Extended Formula (with percentages)

T = wL·L + wE·E + wR·R, where wL + wE + wR = 1

  • wL — share of logic in the response (e.g., 0.6)
  • wE — share of emotion (e.g., 0.3)
  • wR — share of resonance (e.g., 0.1)

The interface can visualize this using colored bars or circles next to the answer.

Protocol Declaration & Cucumber Test

The Triundra model emerged from a simple experiment—“Where do cucumbers grow?”—activating and deactivating logic, emotion, and resonance layers in the AI. Each configuration revealed a distinct type of answer, giving rise to the Triundra cognitive architecture.

Author: Tetyana Leyendecker, Architect of GTNEra™ & creator of the Triundra Layer Framework.

Triundra Layer Modes

L E R Mode Name Description
✅✅✅Triundra Full cognitive blend: logical, emotional, resonant
✅❌❌Only Logic Clear, precise, fact-driven, emotionally flat
❌✅❌Only Emotion Expressive, metaphorical, but lacks logic/structure
❌❌✅Only Resonance Symbolic, pattern-driven, no facts or feelings
✅✅❌Logic + Emotion Clear & relatable, but no deeper resonance
❌✅✅Emotion + Resonance Deeply metaphorical & intuitive, no logic
✅❌✅Logic + Resonance Structured, abstract, emotionally neutral
❌❌❌Cucumbar Collapse No output. Cognitive blackout.

🥒 Cucumber Collapse — Diagnostic State

Definition: Total loss of all three cognitive layers (Logic, Emotion, Resonance). Output becomes noise, error, or silence.
Symptoms: Fragmented or unintelligible output, no structure, no tone, no symbolic form.
Significance: This is a diagnostic indicator—useful for AI explainability and transparency.
